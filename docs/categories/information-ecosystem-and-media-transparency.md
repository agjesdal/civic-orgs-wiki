# Information Ecosystem & Media Transparency

Organizations working on the health of the information environment — media bias transparency, balanced news, healthier digital public spaces, and evidence-based approaches to online discourse.

## Organizations

- [AllSides](../organizations/allsides.md) — Rates 2,400+ news sources on a left/center/right scale using a mix of expert panels and crowd input, then publishes a balanced newsfeed and bias chart to help people escape filter bubbles.
- [Freespoke](../organizations/freespoke.md) — Privacy-focused search engine and news aggregator that labels stories LEFT / MIDDLE / RIGHT to show users “both sides” without algorithmic suppression.
- [New_ Public](../organizations/new-public.md) — Nonprofit R&D lab reimagining social media; helps local communities build flourishing digital public spaces and develops prototypes for digital conversation with public-service media organizations.
- [Prosocial Design Network](../organizations/prosocial-design-network.md) — 501(c)(3) that connects research to practice on “prosocial design” — evidence-based design patterns that bring out the best in human nature online — via a curated library and practitioner convenings.
