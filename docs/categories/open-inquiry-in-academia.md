# Open Inquiry in Academia

Organizations focused on viewpoint diversity, academic freedom, free expression, and constructive disagreement within higher education and research institutions.

## Organizations

- [Constructive Dialogue Institute](../organizations/constructive-dialogue-institute.md) — Psychology-based nonprofit (formerly OpenMind) building educational tools — including the Perspectives blended learning program — that equip students, faculty, and workplaces with the mindset and skills for cross-difference dialogue.
- [FIRE (Foundation for Individual Rights and Expression)](../organizations/fire.md) — Civil liberties nonprofit defending free speech, academic freedom, and due process — historically on campus, broadened in 2022 to general First Amendment work in courts and culture.
- [Heterodox Academy](../organizations/heterodox-academy.md) — Advances open inquiry, viewpoint diversity, and constructive disagreement in higher education through faculty/student membership and campus reform tools.
- [Sustained Dialogue Institute](../organizations/sustained-dialogue-institute.md) — Nonprofit applying the Sustained Dialogue peace process (developed by diplomat Harold Saunders) through a campus network, workplaces, and communities; defines dialogue as “listening deeply enough to be changed by what you learn.”
