# Regenerative & Systems-Change Civic Movements

Organizations oriented around systemic transformation, regenerative approaches, and civic innovation that works at the level of whole systems rather than single issues.

## Organizations

- [OpenCivics](../organizations/opencivics.md) — Fiscally sponsored movement (under Buckminster Fuller Institute) of “civic stewards” practicing open civic innovation; oriented around metacrisis, bioregionalism, regenerative finance, and web3 coordination.
