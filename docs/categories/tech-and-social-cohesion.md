# Tech & Social Cohesion (Prosocial Design)

Organizations working to shift technology design toward social cohesion, trust, and constructive interaction — the emerging field of prosocial design and tech-for-cohesion.

## Organizations

- [Council on Technology and Social Cohesion](../organizations/council-on-technology-and-social-cohesion.md) — Cross-sector coalition bringing together technologists, peacebuilders, academics, and policy influencers to shift technology design toward social cohesion, civic trust, and reduced polarization — organized around hubs including a Deliberative Tech and an LLMs & AI Agents stream.
- [New_ Public](../organizations/new-public.md) — Nonprofit R&D lab reimagining social media; helps local communities build flourishing digital public spaces and develops prototypes for digital conversation with public-service media organizations.
- [Plurality Institute](../organizations/plurality-institute.md) — Research hub for “plurality” — technology for human cooperation at scale — convening academics, technologists, and civic leaders; builds tools including BridgingBot, an LLM-powered moderator that fosters constructive online dialogue.
- [Prosocial Design Network](../organizations/prosocial-design-network.md) — 501(c)(3) that connects research to practice on “prosocial design” — evidence-based design patterns that bring out the best in human nature online — via a curated library and practitioner convenings.
