# AllSides

**Domain:** allsides.com  
**Categories:** [Information Ecosystem & Media Transparency](../categories/information-ecosystem-and-media-transparency.md)

## Mission

Rates 2,400+ news sources on a left/center/right scale using a mix of expert panels and crowd input, then publishes a balanced newsfeed and bias chart to help people escape filter bubbles.

## Notes

Methodologically transparent peer of Freespoke without the partisan-founder concerns; rated Center in blind bias surveys.
