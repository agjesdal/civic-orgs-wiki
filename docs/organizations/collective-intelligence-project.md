## Mission

501(c)(3) building tools and methods for democratic oversight of AI; runs “Alignment Assemblies” (often via Polis) that surface public input on AI behavior in partnership with frontier labs and governments.

Sits at the intersection of LLMs and democratic input; has partnered with Anthropic (Collective Constitutional AI), OpenAI, and the UK AISI.
