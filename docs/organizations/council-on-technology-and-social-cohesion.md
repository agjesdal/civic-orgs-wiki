# Council on Technology and Social Cohesion

**Domain:** techandsocialcohesion.org  
**Categories:** [Tech & Social Cohesion (Prosocial Design)](../categories/tech-and-social-cohesion.md)  
**Date added:** 2026-05-14

## Mission

Cross-sector coalition bringing together technologists, peacebuilders, academics, and policy influencers to shift technology design toward social cohesion, civic trust, and reduced polarization — organized around hubs including a Deliberative Tech and an LLMs & AI Agents stream.

## Notes

Surfaced via the Plurality Institute site; co-produced the “LLM Map.” A field-catalyst and convener rather than a product builder. Founding member is Search for Common Ground.
