**Domain:** democracyrd.org

## Mission

International network of ~95 organizations advancing deliberative democracy — primarily citizens’ assemblies and sortition-based processes — through annual convenings and peer learning.

## Notes

The practitioner network for deliberative democracy globally; newDemocracy Foundation is its co-founder/parent.
