# FIRE (Foundation for Individual Rights and Expression)

**Domain:** thefire.org  
**Categories:** [Open Inquiry in Academia](../categories/open-inquiry-in-academia.md)  
**Date added:** 2026-05-14

## Mission

Civil liberties nonprofit defending free speech, academic freedom, and due process — historically on campus, broadened in 2022 to general First Amendment work in courts and culture.

## Notes

Operationally distinct from Heterodox Academy (litigation/advocacy vs. faculty membership and culture change). Funded heavily by Stand Together Trust, Koch Foundation, Bradley, and Templeton.
