## Mission

Nonprofit interdisciplinary research collective building standards and infrastructure for digital self-governance; ran a Mina Foundation–funded cohort that brought 21 deliberative-tool teams to minimum viable interoperability.

## Notes

Surfaced via the Plurality Institute site. Its interoperability standards for AI-for-democracy tools are now being adopted by the Government of Scotland. Funded in part by Common (One Project) and Project Liberty.
