# More in Common

**Domain:** moreincommon.com  
**Categories:** [Bridging Political Divides](../categories/bridging-political-divides.md)  
**Date added:** 2026-05-14

## Mission

International research nonprofit (US, UK, France, Germany, Poland, Spain, Brazil) that studies the psychology of polarization and “perception gaps,” then partners with civil society, media, faith, and business actors to counter “us vs. them” narratives.

## Notes

Provides the evidence base much of the bridging field draws on (e.g., the Hidden Tribes report).
