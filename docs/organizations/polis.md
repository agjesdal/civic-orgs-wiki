# Polis

**Domain:** pol.is  
**Categories:** [Civic Tech for Deliberation & Participation](../categories/civic-tech-for-deliberation.md)  
**Date added:** 2026-05-14

## Mission

Open-source, AI-assisted conversation platform (stewarded by the nonprofit Computational Democracy Project) that surfaces areas of consensus and division in large-group deliberation; used at national scale in Taiwan.

## Notes

Used by CIP for Alignment Assemblies. Relevant as both a tool and an org to know.
