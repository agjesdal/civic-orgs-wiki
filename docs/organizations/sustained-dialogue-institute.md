## Mission

Nonprofit applying the Sustained Dialogue peace process (developed by diplomat Harold Saunders) through a campus network, workplaces, and communities; defines dialogue as “listening deeply enough to be changed by what you learn.”

Distinct diplomacy-derived methodology with a strong campus footprint.
